Bank Profile
A saga of vision and enterprise

It has been a long and eventful journey of more than a century, Bank of KC in India The parent Bank has International presence in 94 overseas offices spanning 17 countries. Starting in 1908 from a small building in KC to its new hi-rise and hi-tech KC Corporate Centre in Mumbai, is a saga of vision, enterprise, financial prudence and corporate governance. It is a story scripted in corporate wisdom and social pride. It is a story crafted in private capital, princely patronage and state ownership. It is a story of ordinary bankers and their extraordinary contribution in the ascent of Bank of KC to the formidable heights of corporate glory.
